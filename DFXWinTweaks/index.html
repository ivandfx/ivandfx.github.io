Farewell, DFX WinTweaks

Thanks to everyone for the continued support - March 21, 2024

A while ago, I announced that the development for DFX WinTweaks (previously DFX Tweaker) would cease.

DFX WinTweaks was just a mini script based on AikonCWD's Win10 Script originally, and over the time, it changed so much that nowadays, 99% of the code it's not what AikonCWD originally did, and was mostly mine. And that is what made me go nuts and release updates way less often.

During development, I was getting my free time (also study time) cut out due to me being unable to sleep properly while knowing all the bugs that I needed to fix even when a new version was just released.

And I'm not a good programmer, heck, I don't really like writing that much if I have to hurt my brain THAT hard finding ways to fix stuff.

To say it quicker, DFX WinTweaks is no longer in development, thanks to everyone who believed in the project since 2022 <3

I have provided new downloads for the latest versions that were released (and the latest development build)
