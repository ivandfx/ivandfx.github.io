About DFX WinTweaks

And why it exists.

DFX WinTweaks exists because I needed a tool to disable Windows Update and Defender, and when I came across AikonCWD W10 Script, I was so pleased...

However, the script needed an internet connection for vast functionality and it was a bit outdated, so I thought I needed to do something like that, but without any internet right out of the box. So the idea of making this project came up, of course, being as transparent as possible about the existence of Aikon and its fantastic script.



Development

Yeah, sure... DFX WinTweaks could have more features from months ago? But hey, I'm a newbie developer (that's why I use VBScript, mainly). I'm trying to do the best I can, and since I don't actually get any reports or issues on GitHub, it's hard for me to find and resolve those issues easily.
