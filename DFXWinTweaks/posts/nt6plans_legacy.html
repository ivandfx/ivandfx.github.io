DFX Tweaker | NT6 Edition has changed my plans

February 15, 2023


Remember when I talked about the DFX Tweaker end of support on Windows 7 and 8.x?

Ok, so I made a new version from scratch (Release 2.0) and it changed my mind. Now I can update two separate editions of DFX Tweaker when needed, and not having the [checkNT10] code destroyed by a full working menu on it.

Basically, DFX Tweaker NT6 will be supported until this project gets completely finished (or abandoned, whatever happens first)
