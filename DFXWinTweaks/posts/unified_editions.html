Standard and NT6 are now unified - July 20, 2023


A while ago, I announced the addition of both Standard and NT6 in the same download, but with a small caveat...

The Standard edition would have a higher version number and the NT6 one will be the "little brother" with a smaller version number. And that is about to change.

Starting on DFX WinTweaks 3.2, both editions will have the same version number, just to avoid confusion and simplify everything:P
