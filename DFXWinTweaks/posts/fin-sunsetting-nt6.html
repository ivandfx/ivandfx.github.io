DFX WinTweaks | Sunsetting NT6, finally

Windows 7/8.x Last Chance - August 22, 2023


Do you remember the first time that DFX WinTweaks (well, DFX Tweaker at the time) lost support for Windows 7/8.x and one of my friends was upset about it so much he decided to continue developing for it? (with some help of mine of course)

Well, that's about to end in the last moments of 2023, because DFX WinTweaks 3.6 will be the first version in a long time to not have support for legacy versions of Windows, as you may have seen on the Release info page.

However, version 3.5 will be based on ESR (Extended Support Release) wich means that 3.5 will be supported and will get new features and bugfixes for 2 months starting at the moment when version 3.6 is released.

For example, if DFX WinTweaks 3.6 is released on December 15 of 2023, version 3.5 will get new updates until February 15 of 2024. Simple as that.

And how I'll do it? Well, when 3.5 ESR loses support, a new (and smaller) button will be added to download the latest version of 3.5 ESR


I know this may not be as likeable as some people wanted, but everyone has to know that older operating systems (not only speaking of Windows) can be a potential target for malware and things like that.

Also, if you have the know-how, you can make your own version of DFX WinTweaks and distribute it with the same license and giving credit of the developer (I would really like AikonCWD being credited too, because DFX WinTweaks wouldn't have been possible without his original work).

After all, I would like to continue supporting Windows 7/8.x, but it's hard for me to develop for older operating systems, and with the size of this project, it's not worth the extra time.

Thanks to everyone for understanding.
