Regarding use of AI

22-07-2025

Let’s be honest, I didn’t make this website 100% by myself — but it’s probably not as bad as you think...

I used artificial intelligence to create pretty much all the JavaScript on the site, which, in short, is the part responsible for the site’s advanced functionality — like sharing posts to Twitter or Bluesky, the music player for ‘Amor Loco’ and ‘Gritos Rotos’, and other internal features.

And why? Very simple: because I don’t know much JavaScript, and yet I absolutely needed it.

At the very least, as I manually tweaked and handled it to some extent, I was gradually learning.


In addition, AI helped clean up and restructure the CSS (the design part) when it started turning into spaghetti code, with lots of duplicated or conflicting rules — in short, things that were making it hard for me to keep working on the site.

And that’s really what it’s about: using AI to make things "easier," and if you put in the effort, you can learn along the way.

I’m one of those who firmly believes that AI should be used to speed up processes — not replace them.


And with that said, this is the end of the first post on the new website. So — what do you think? Do you like it?
