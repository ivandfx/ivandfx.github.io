Regarding use of AI

22-07-2025

Let’s be honest, I didn’t make this website 100% by myself… but it’s probably not as bad as you might think.

I used artificial intelligence to create a good chunk of the JavaScript on the site — which, to sum it up, handles the more advanced functionality, like sharing posts to Twitter or Bluesky, the music player for ‘Amor Loco’ and ‘Gritos Rotos’, among other internal things.

And why? Simple: because I don’t know much JavaScript, and yet, I needed it no matter what.

At least, as I manually edited and adjusted things, little by little, I was learning.


AI also helped me clean up and restructure the CSS (the design part) when it started to turn into spaghetti code — with duplicated styles and conflicts that made it harder to keep working on the site.

At the end of the day, that’s what it’s about: using AI to make things easier and, if you’re invested, learning something along the way.

I firmly believe that AI should be used to speed things up, not to replace the process entirely.

So the answer you’re probably looking for is… No, this website wasn’t made by an AI — I used the AI to help me learn and build it myself.
